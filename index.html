About Me

Engineer. Researcher.
Builder.

I'm a Computer Engineering graduate from Birzeit University, Palestine, with a passion that runs from the transistor level all the way to production-grade AI systems. I thrive where hardware meets intelligence.

My work spans RTL design and verification (SystemVerilog, UVM), machine learning and deep learning (classification, retrieval, multimodal), computer vision, NLP, embedded systems, and applied cryptography.

I've designed pipelined RISC processors, built multimodal classifiers from scratch, implemented encryption algorithms, and shipped end-to-end ML pipelines — driven by a belief that a great engineer understands the full stack.

I care about professional ethics, effective communication, and the real-world impact of engineering solutions. Actively looking for roles in ML engineering, hardware verification, embedded AI, and software development.
